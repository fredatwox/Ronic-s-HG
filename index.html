Ronic's Foundation

The Ronic's Foundation is dedicated to improving health outcomes for girls and women in underserved communities through education, resources, and advocacy.

Since its founding in 2015, the foundation has reached over 200 girls and women across ghana with health education programs, sanitary products, and mentorship opportunities.

Our current campaign aims to provide menstrual health education and sustainable hygiene products to 10,000 school-aged girls in rural communities.
